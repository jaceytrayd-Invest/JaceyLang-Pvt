About the Author

JaceyLang is the public-facing practice of John Christopher. The folio to which access has been granted comprises commissioned works undertaken under private engagement.

Each piece proceeds from a defined brief, and from the understanding that language is not ornament, but function. Precision is assumed; haste finds no place here.

These are neither drafts nor speculative samples. They are delivered works — considered, resolved, and shared in discretion, to be read in that same spirit.

Each volume in this library represents a work produced under private commission. Those with access may select a title to read.
